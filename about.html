Hi, I'm Luan Ye.

I'm a part-time student in the Master's of Information & Data Science (MIDS) program at UC Berkeley, with an undergraduate background in mechanical engineering from UCLA. I'm passionate about applying machine learning and statistical analysis to solve real-world problems, especially to improve quality of life. My domains of interest include healthcare, utilities, and finance.

I currently work full time at the Los Angeles Department of Water & Power (LADWP), a large municipal utility serving over 4 million customers. My role lies at the intersection of data and traditional engineering, where I help improve our maintenance and asset management programs for water operations. I interface with field operators and leverage data analysis skills (Python, SQL, Power BI, etc.) to enhance our maintenance strategy through data-driven decisions.

I am skilled in traditional data science skills (Python, SQL, Power BI, Tableau) and am equipped with an engineering mindset. I am extremely detail oriented and thoroughly enjoy the process of deriving insights from messy data.
